Gallery


This page is dedicated to my best folds of other peoples’ designs. As much as I like designing my own origami, it’s always nice to see how other people overcome this challenge, whether that be through diagrams, crease patterns, or otherwise. It’s also a great way to test myself against the standards of other people, and to work on certain aspects of my folding, such as shaping, to implement in my own designs. In the end, though, I fold other peoples’ designs for the pure joy of seeing some of these amazing designs come to life in my own hands.
